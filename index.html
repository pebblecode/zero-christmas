ZERO-XMAS

Zero Christmas

"Every idiot who goes about with 'Merry Christmas' on his lips, should be boiled with his own pudding, and buried with a stake of holly through his heart."
-Ebenezer Scrooge

If like me and Mr Scrooge you'd like a little less Christmas in your life you need Zero Christmas!

Zero Christmas is a javascript bootmarklet that removes all christmasy images, text, and colours from a web-page.

Just drag the circle below to you bookmarks bar. Then click the bookmark on any page you find offensively festive.
